# Business Recommendations

These are the recommendations I would present after completing the analysis.

## 1. Improve Stock Availability

Categories with high revenue and high out-of-stock count should be restocked first. These products are likely losing sales because customers cannot buy them when needed.

## 2. Watch High Discount Products

Products with very high discounts should be reviewed. Discounts can improve sales, but they can also reduce profit if used too often.

## 3. Promote Best Value Products

Products with low price per gram can be promoted as value-for-money items. This can help increase customer trust and basket size.

## 4. Reduce Overstocking

Categories with high inventory value but low revenue should be checked. Too much inventory in slow-moving categories can block money.

## 5. Build Reorder Alerts

Products that are out of stock or have very low available quantity should be added to a reorder alert list.

## Final Recommendation

My main recommendation is to increase inventory for fast-moving categories and create simple reorder rules for products that are frequently out of stock.
